Who I am

I'm a pedagogue — and in the corporate world, where people talk a lot about training and very little about learning, that's exactly what sets me apart. I've spent 15 years designing learning experiences: I crossed from the classroom and university educational innovation into organizational development, across industries as different as global manufacturing and the tequila industry. I work with two engines: I go deep into theory to understand what's happening at the psychological, social, and systemic level, and then I put it up against reality to build experiences that make sense. People tell me I "seem like a teacher" — I take it as a compliment: I make complex things simple. And while I can't promise that people will learn (the will to learn can't be imposed), I do promise to design every experience with the same love I bring to learning something new myself.
